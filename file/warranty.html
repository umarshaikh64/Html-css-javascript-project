What if support is required for a product bought using International Warranty? Such services can be availed for a nominal charge.


How can I obtain warranty from Mehran Mobile? You just need to bring in the machine to us and we will take care of it. In case of products bought using International Warranty only, you need to take it to the manufacturer's service center. What is covered under warranty? We warrant that each product is free from defects in materials and workmanship. Warranty covers:




Any manufacturing defect. Any internal fault arising out of manufacturing defect.

This warranty covers the following and is limited to:


Screen replacement if the number of dead pixels are more than 11. Keyboard and Optical drive contain a warranty of only 6 months.


What is not covered under warranty?


Warranty does not cover:


Less than 11 dead pixels in the screen. Battery timing, battery functionality and Power Adapter functionality. Minor cosmetic imperfections that do not affect the performance and usability of the product. Any software programs, whether pre-loaded or shipped with the product, or installed subsequently. Failure resulting from misuse, user neglect, accident, modification, unsuitable physical or operating environment, or improper maintenance by user. Any damage due to non-branded, unknown products integrated or added afterwards, including those that have been produced by the original manufacturer.
